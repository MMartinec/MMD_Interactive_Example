My Trips

The My Trips screen displays the users next upcoming trip, as well as a list of future trips.

The Next Trip box should list information about the next trip including: The date of the trip, the estimated time of departure, the number of passengers, and the trip route. There is also a More Details link where the user can continue on to their next action.

The Future Trips box should display at least 6 trips that will be coming up after the Next Trip. The only information required for these trips is their trip route and the date of each trip.
